Engagement Categories

Students are grouped into horizontal bands based on their primary engagement category.

Academic: Research, lectures, study groups (blue band)
Social: Clubs, campus events, community service (yellow band)
Professional: Career workshops, networking, industry panels (teal band)
Cultural: Arts, music, diversity events (gray band)
Athletic: Sports, fitness, team activities (red band)

Engagement Styles

Each icon's position and shape indicates the student's engagement style.

Sampler: Tries a variety of different activities (scattered positions, circle shape)
Specialist: Focuses deeply on one category (clustered tightly in category band, square shape)
Super Connector: Bridges between multiple categories (often spans category boundaries, cross shape)
Selective: Participates in few carefully chosen activities (forms tight clusters, diamond shape)

Visualization Features

Other visual elements provide additional information:

Icon Size: Larger icons indicate more engagement events
Connections: Lines show students who participated in the same events
Highlight: Selected or hovered students have white borders
Evolution View: Shows how engagement changes over the school year
